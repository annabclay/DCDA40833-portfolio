    Semester Reflection

    What I learned: What was new to me when working on this lab was the .gitkeep and .gitignore placeholder files. I can't remember if we used these last semester, but they are unfamiliar to me. I learned that once the images are added to the folder, they are no longer necessary.

    Challenges I overcame: When working on this lab, the challenge I faced was VS Code not opening my files. I tried deleting the repository and redownloading it, shutting down my computer, deleting VS Code, watching YouTube videos, and asking for help. After working with my AI assistant on VS code it helped me change some of the viewing settings. The AI assistant reset the explorer view and expanded folder view, refreshed the files explorer, reset the explorer view container location, and collapsed and reset the explorer folders.

    How I grew: Through this lab, I learned how to troubleshoot technical issues more effectively instead of getting stuck or overwhelmed. Working through the VS Code issues helped me become more confident in using development tools and relying on problem-solving strategies when something does not work as expected.

    Looking ahead: This semester, I am most looking forward to making a website that reflects my personal interests/hobbies, with lots of graphics and features that I have not worked with before.
